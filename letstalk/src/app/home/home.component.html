About Us

Let's Talk Psychological and Consultation Services (YPCSKOREA) supports and enhances your health and wellness by providing comprehensive and holistic mental health services. We strive to assist you by providing support during times of difficulties, providing advocacy when needed, facilitating problem resolution, enhancing coping skills, promoting personal growth, and supporting academic/professional success.

  • Helpful information on anxiety, depression (and other issues they may be struggling with like leaving school or getting on with their parents).
  • Quick steps to help build healthy mental wellbeing.
  • Healthy behaviours to build resilience.
  • A moderated forum for young people to share stories and experiences and provide peer-to-peer support.

We promote and develop safe, ethical and competent practice in counselling and psychotherapy. See how we are raising professional standards, supporting clients and dealing with complaints about poor or unethical practice.
